About

My name is Juan Gabriel Salamanca-Leon, born on June 11, 1990 in Ponce, Puerto Rico. Raised in Lake Wales, FL since the age of 4 and currently living in Orlando, FL. I am currently a cast member for the Walt Disney World company since 2012 and status at Typhoon Lagoon Water Park. I also work as a certified substitute teacher for Polk County School Board and a technician for Micro-Guard Security Systems. If that doesn't keep me busy then, in my spare time I like to build models and play guitar. Mostly anything I can work my hands on that gives me a challenge.

Part of the UCF Coding Boot Camp and set to finish with a Full Stack Web Deveolper certification at the end of the program.
